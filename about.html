About Airmaxx Hvac (India)

The company was started in the year 1987 with its headquarters located at Adyar, Chennai-600020 as a consultant's office. The erstwhile proprietor of the firm is Graduate Engineer specialized in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning field and his industrial experience gained during his tenure as Engineer from the Air Conditioning and Refrigeration companies in India and the overseas experience was helpful in a good take off for the company. Today companies activities are centered on Manufacturing, Sales, Installation, and Servicing of Central/Cleanroom Air conditioning, Industrial Chilling Plants, and Cold Storage products designed for customer on a turnkey basis and render with integrated setup of the Service/maintenance after-sales & service
